About Us

Connecting Alumni. Empowering Institutions.

Our Mission

We aim to build a centralized platform that bridges alumni and institutions across India — enabling mentorship, donations, job opportunities, and lifelong engagement.

Our Story

Born from the need to solve fragmented alumni data and low engagement, our platform was designed to be modular, scalable, and visually welcoming. We believe in practical impact through thoughtful design and robust architecture.

What We Offer

Alumni Directory

Search and connect with fellow alumni across batches and departments.

Mentorship Scheduling

Offer or request mentorship sessions with easy scheduling tools.

Job & Event Board

Post and discover opportunities and events relevant to your network.

Donation Portal

Support your institution with secure, purpose-driven contributions.

Impact So Far

Over 5,000 alumni registered, ₹12L in donations raised, 300+ mentorship hours logged, and growing every day.
