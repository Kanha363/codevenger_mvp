About Us

Learn about our mission and the impact of the Alumni Association.

Our Mission

To foster a strong, lifelong connection between the institution and its alumni, creating a network of mutual support, professional growth, and mentorship.

Our Story

Established in 2025, the alumnusHub was founded by a passionate group of graduates aiming to keep the spirit of Institution alive long after graduation. We strive to be the central point for all alumni activity, offering resources, events, and opportunities to give back.
